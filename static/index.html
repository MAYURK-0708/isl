Hello

Wave your hand from side to side with your palm facing outward. Keep your fingers together and move your hand in a friendly waving motion.

Steps:

  1. Raise your dominant hand to shoulder height
  2. Keep your palm facing outward
  3. Wave your hand side to side 2-3 times

How are you

Point to yourself, then extend your hand toward the other person with a questioning expression on your face.

Steps:

  1. Point your index finger to your chest
  2. Extend both hands forward, palms up
  3. Show a questioning facial expression

Thank you

Touch your chin or lips with your fingertips, then move your hand forward and down toward the person you're thanking.

Steps:

  1. Touch your chin with fingertips
  2. Move your hand forward and downward
  3. End with palm facing up
üëå

Alright

Make an 'OK' sign with your thumb and index finger forming a circle, with other fingers extended upward.

Steps:

  1. Touch thumb tip to index finger tip
  2. Extend other three fingers upward
  3. Hold hand at chest level

Good morning

Sign 'good' followed by 'morning' - bring your flat hand from chin outward, then raise your arm upward like the sun rising.

Steps:

  1. Place flat hand near chin
  2. Move hand forward (sign for 'good')
  3. Raise opposite arm upward (sunrise)
üåû

Good afternoon

Similar to 'good morning' but the arm is held at a higher angle to represent the sun at midday.

Steps:

  1. Place flat hand near chin
  2. Move hand forward (sign for 'good')
  3. Hold opposite arm horizontally (noon position)

Our Mission

In India, there are approximately 5 million deaf individuals but only around 250 certified sign language interpreters - that's 1 interpreter for every 20,000 deaf people. Our mission is to bridge this communication gap using AI technology, enabling deaf individuals to communicate more easily with the hearing world without requiring a human interpreter.
